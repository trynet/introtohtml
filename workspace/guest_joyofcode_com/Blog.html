How Much?

by Bud Kraus | Published August 13

If you are new to gardening you are going to wonder, "How much should I water? How much should I fertilize"? The truth of the matter is I do it by experience and not by science but that can get me into trouble.

I like to think that I make gardens like the way my Grandmother cooked meals. A pinch of this, a handful of that. Cooking by feel worked for her and it has worked pretty well for me.

Speaking of fertilizer, I try to keep chemicals out of the garden but I am not a purist about this. Once I put way too much fertilizer around the tomato plants and I proved too much is no good either. The overdose resulted in plants that turned yellow and, grew tall and did not yield as many tomatoes as they should have.
